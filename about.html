About Strata Nova AS

We make sense of human experience through data, and make data meaningful for humans.

Strata Nova AS operates at the intersection of psychology, neuroscience, machine learning, and data science. We solve methodological problems that arise when analyzing complex human data — bringing rigorous standards to every step of the research process, while embracing not knowing as the first step toward knowing.

Our Unique Value

Our dual expertise combines rigorous psychological science with computational knowledge, identifying methodological gaps that specialists in either field alone might miss. We don't just analyze data; we translate between human complexity and computational insights.

Consider your HR system's performance scores. Most take these at face value — but how was performance actually measured? What if the error margin is very large? If you're making hiring or promotion decisions based on flawed measurement, you might be letting your best talent go because you trusted the numbers without understanding how they were created. The same applies to Big Five personality results or any human data: even skilled data scientists can build sophisticated models, but if the underlying data collection was flawed, the analysis will inherit those problems. We help bridge this gap by understanding both how human data should be collected and how it should be analyzed.

What We Do

We operate with two complementary missions: Specialized Consulting that delivers services combining psychological rigor with computational methods, and a Collaborative Platform connecting researchers, students, and businesses for meaningful partnerships.

Our approach integrates three principles. Evidence-Based Practice: We integrate rigorous research evidence with domain expertise, tailored to your specific organizational context rather than following generic "best practices," grounding business decisions in solid research while ensuring they remain practical and contextually appropriate. Open Science: We document what works and what doesn't, what we know for certain and what remains uncertain — this transparency creates better learning opportunities and more trustworthy results. Collaborative by Design: Rather than waiting for the perfect business plan, we've chosen to start now and evolve through meaningful work and partnerships.
