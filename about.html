About Strata Nova AS

We make sense of human experience through data, and make data meaningful for humans.

Strata Nova is a research startup working at the intersection of data, machine learning, and research methods.

We take observations, turn them into data, extract what matters, and translate it back into something people can use. That loop — and how to do it rigorously — is what drives our work.

Our team combines backgrounds in cognitive neuroscience, psychology, computer science, and business analytics. We care as much about the method as the result — because how you collect, structure, and analyse data shapes everything downstream.

We work openly and build on what we learn.
