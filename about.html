About Strata Nova AS

We make sense of human experience through data, and make data meaningful for humans.

Strata Nova AS operates at the intersection of psychology and data science, with a strong background in neuroscience and machine learning. We solve methodological problems across domains that arise when analyzing complex human data — while embracing uncertainty as the path to knowledge.

Our Unique Value

Our dual expertise combines behavioral science with analytical rigor, identifying methodological gaps that specialists in either field alone might miss. We translate between human complexity and quantitative insights, understanding both how human data should be collected and how it should be analyzed. This matters because flawed measurement leads to flawed decisions — from HR systems to personality assessments to advanced machine learning models.

What We Do

We operate with two complementary missions:

  1. Specialized Consulting: Integrates psychological principles with data-driven methods
  2. Collaborative Platform: Connects researchers, students, and businesses

Our approach: Research-grounded practice tailored to your context. Open science transparency documenting what works, what doesn't, and what's uncertain. Partnership-driven design that evolves through meaningful collaboration.
