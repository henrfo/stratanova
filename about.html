About Strata Nova

Strata Nova AS is an innovative initiative exploring the intersection of psychology, neuroscience, machine learning, data science, and human experience. We create a space where knowledge across disciplines connects and evolves in real time.

Our approach reaffirms science's core values that have often become obscured in practice. While science inherently values openness, today's research landscape often separates discovery from application, experts from learners, and process from results. We're working to reconnect these elements by: embracing the scientific process with all its messy iterations; making specialized knowledge accessible beyond academia; and translating theoretical insights into practical solutions for real-world challenges.

We deliberately make our entire research journey visible — sharing uncertainties, false starts, and breakthroughs alike. By documenting this process, we create learning opportunities not just for ourselves but for anyone interested in these intersecting fields, returning to the collaborative spirit that makes science thrive.

Though we began as a student-led project, our vision extends beyond academic boundaries. We believe the most valuable insights emerge when disciplines overlap and knowledge flows freely between research environments and professional contexts, breaking down the artificial barriers that often slow innovation.

At Strata Nova, we invite others to join this journey through cognitive science, data analysis, and technological innovation. Our growing community welcomes those who share our commitment to practicing science as it was meant to be — where methods, data, and insights are shared as they develop, not just when perfected.

Above all, Strata Nova embodies curiosity in action — a place to think, share, and build while still figuring things out. We're not just studying the application and intersection of mind and machine; we're actively translating this knowledge into meaningful contributions.
